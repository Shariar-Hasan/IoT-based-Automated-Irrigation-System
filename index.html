About the Project

Our project, "IoT-based Automated Irrigation System & Mobile App for Tea Garden" is a groundbreaking solution designed to revolutionize agriculture by integrating IoT technology into irrigation processes.

By automating irrigation based on real-time data analysis, we aim to:

  • Optimize water usage
  • Enhance crop yield
  • Reduce the workload for farmers
  • Promote sustainable farming practices

Key Features

Water Efficiency

Our system ensures precise water distribution, reducing waste and conserving this precious resource.

Remote Monitoring

Monitor your tea garden in real-time through our user-friendly mobile app, keeping you in control, no matter where you are.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable agriculture, contributing to both the environment and your long-term profitability.
